About

Builder's instinct, still in progress.

I'm a Computer Science undergraduate at NED University of Engineering and Technology, Karachi, currently maintaining a 3.6/4.0 CGPA. My interests span game development, desktop applications, database design, and building software that solves real accessibility and operational problems — not just coursework exercises.

Every project I take on is a chance to go one layer deeper: writing a 2D game engine loop in raw C, designing a normalized relational database from scratch across eleven entities, or wiring speech recognition and Dijkstra's algorithm together into a navigation aid for visually impaired students. I care about clean architecture and code that's still readable months later.

Outside the codebase, I've helped plan and run large-scale university tech events for 500+ attendees — which taught me as much about coordination under pressure as any class did.

I'm still an undergraduate — Class of 2028 — with two more years of coursework ahead. Everything here comes from academic projects, coursework, and volunteering, not a professional track record yet, and I'm looking for internships and collaborations to build on it.
